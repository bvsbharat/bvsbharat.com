## What Actually Matters

Rather than declaring one "best" framework, here's the truth: **there's no single winner‚Äîbut there are clear winners depending on what you're actually trying to build.**

Different tools excel in different areas:
- **Multi-agent coordination** ‚Äî How well can multiple agents work together?
- **State management** ‚Äî How are conversations and context maintained?
- **Deployment options** ‚Äî What are your hosting and scaling requirements?
- **Architectural philosophy** ‚Äî Does the framework match your mental model?
- **Community support** ‚Äî How active is development and community engagement?
